AirCam

(Nuxt 2)

AirCam is a platform that tries to promote the Sardinian territory by streaming with tiny little cameras placed in points of interests. While the main website has been rebuilt multiple times from scratch using the vanilla Vue, we ultimately chose to move on Nuxt 2 for its SSR capabilities, to solve some of our SEO issues. The site is also a Progressive Web App and can be installed on supported devices. In order to manage the growing content available on the platform we chose to rely on a Strapi instance, with the GraphQL plugin, as our backend.
