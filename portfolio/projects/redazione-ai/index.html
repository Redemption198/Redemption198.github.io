Redazione AI

(Nuxt 4 + PrimeVue + Laravel + n8n)

2025 – Ongoing (Web App)

Redazione AI is a modern editorial management platform built with Nuxt 4 and PrimeVue on the frontend, Laravel for backend services, and n8n for workflow automation and AI-based article & editorial plan generation.

Note: Redazione AI provides tools for managing editorial teams, generating content plans automatically with AI, and streamlining publishing workflows across multiple social platforms.

Article Generator

AI-powered article creation driven by n8n workflows, with support for multiple input sources and export formats.

Dashboard

The admin dashboard allows editors to manage multiple redazioni (editorial groups), credits, users, and subscription plans. It’s powered by Laravel APIs and presented with PrimeVue’s dynamic data tables.

Editorial Plan Generator

A unique feature of Redazione AI is the generation of editorial plans. By integrating n8n workflows with large language models, editors can generate post schedules, AI-written article drafts, and accompanying images for different platforms.

User Management

User and team management is seamlessly handled within the dashboard. Admins can add editors, assign roles, and manage authentication through Laravel’s secure backend.

Automation with n8n

Behind the scenes, n8n orchestrates workflows that connect AI models and image generators. This makes Redazione AI flexible, extendable, and ready for custom integrations.
